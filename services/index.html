Design Services and Fees

Initial Visit Design Consultation : $50

In-home meeting to meet one on one to determine the scope of the design project and may include a little design advice.

Design Consultation: $75 per hour

Includes accessorizing, paint specifications, rearranging of existing furniture, hanging artwork/pictures. This is all done in-home.

Design Presentation :$75 per hour

Includes a mood board for a room(s) and will include specifications of everything from floor to ceiling. For example selections of carpeting/tile/wood flooring, paint/wallpaper, furniture, lighting, accessories, bathroom/kitchen fixtures, countertops, cabinets, artwork.
