Jaclyn Drum grew up in a suburb of Cleveland, Ohio and received her Bachelor of Science in Human and Consumer Sciences degree in Interior Architecture from Ohio University. She began her career in design as an architectural sales representative for Lamin-Art. Then she moved onto designing model homes and real estate sales centers around the country. She next worked at Kaufman Segal Design in Chicago designing high-end residential and commercial spaces. After that she worked in high-end furniture and lighting sales for Holly Hunt in the Chicago showroom. She opened her own firm in 2017 in Chagrin Falls, a suburb of Cleveland, Ohio. Jaclyn enjoys a more modern space with some traditional flair.

The key to great design is capturing the spirit of the client and the essence of the space.
